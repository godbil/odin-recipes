Pizza

Description

This is an amazing recipe for a delicious Pepperoni Pizza. Yum yum yum! Just talking about the Pizza is making my mouth water. I love this recipe so much because it lets me create an amazing crust and the best tomato sauce that I have ever tasted in my life. I even treated Sir Donald Trump to this Pizza and he was amazed at my recipe, even asking me to become his personal chef! Unfortunately, I declined as I am a single mother with 12 children but I still visit Donnie from time to time. Now, I will share my mythical Pizza Recipe to the world! Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Salt from Utah
  • Unwashed Ripe Tomatoes (From the farm)
  • 3 pounds of Pepperoni
  • Egg
  • 2 Waters
  • Yeast!
  • 12 Crazy Boys
  • 1 Hair
  • 1 Raw Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, yeast, egg and 1 of the 2 waters in a BOWL. Let the YEAST grow.
  2. Kneed the dough until it comes together as a BALL and oil using 5 of the 12 crazy boys.
  3. Let the dough rest for 2-7 business days.
  4. Take the tomatoes and make juice.
  5. Spread raw cheese on dough ball until it looks like a pizza.
  6. Put tomato juice in a cup and drink half, then pour the rest on the dough.
  7. Eat one crazy boy and put the other 6 on the pizza along with the entire stick of pepperoni (wash your pepperoni).
  8. Bake in over at 700 degrees for 30-60 minutes depending on how crazy the boys are.
  9. Take out pizza and pour the remaining cup of water on it. Bake for another 3 minutes to crisp the water.
  10. Take out the pizza and sprinkle with salt from Utah and the hair (preferably blonde hair from Mr. Trump).
  11. Let's Eat!
